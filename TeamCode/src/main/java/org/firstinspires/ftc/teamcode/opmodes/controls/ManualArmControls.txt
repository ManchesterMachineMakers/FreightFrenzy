Manual Arm TeleOp Controls:
    Gamepad 2:
        Left Stick:
            Up: moves arm upwards (away from floor)
            Down: moves arm downwards (towards floor)
        Left Bumper: open left release
        Left Trigger: close left release
        Right Bumper: open right release
        Right Trigger: close left release
        D-Pad:
            Up: slightly move wrist up
            Down: slightly move wrist down
            Right: move wrist up significantly
            Left: move wrist down significantly
        B: reset encoder
